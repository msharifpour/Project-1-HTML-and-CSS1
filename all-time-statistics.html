El Clasico All-Time Statistics

In La Liga, Los Blancos have been dominating the Catalans since 1929. Real Madrid and Barcelona have played 177 La Liga Matches so far. Out of which Real Madrid has won 72 matches while Barcelona has won 71. Meanwhile 34 Matches have ended in a draw.
